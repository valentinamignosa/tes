# TES — Tracing Eastern Sicily’s Networks (9th–6th centuries BCE)

Research project hosted at the Department of Humanities, Ca’ Foscari University of Venice (Apr 2025–Mar 2028).

## About
TES investigates political and social dynamics in south-eastern Sicily and the wider Mediterranean through an integrated approach combining archaeology, epigraphy, and digital humanities.
